Online practice for psychotherapy

Sometimes we realize that something no longer feels right, even though we have endured it for a long time. I offer psychotherapeutic support that can help in exactly these phases to find new ways forward.

Services

Online individual therapy for private patients and self-payers

I support people in very different life situations. Some seek help with anxiety, others struggle with sadness or feel overwhelmed by their experiences. Others want to cope better with stress or understand their personality more deeply. What matters is what moves you and what we would like to look at together.

Online aftercare groups (Psyrena) for rehabilitation participants

In cooperation with partner practices, I offer online aftercare groups as part of the Psyrena program.

If you have taken part in rehabilitation and have already received approval for aftercare, you are welcome to contact me. In this case, the costs are covered by the German pension insurance.
